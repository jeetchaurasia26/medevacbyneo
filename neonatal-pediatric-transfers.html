    Neonatal & Pediatric Transfer

    Safe, Specialized Air Medical Transport for Newborns and Children

    Medevac by NEO provides dedicated Neonatal and Pediatric Air Ambulance Transfer services for critically ill newborns, infants, and children requiring urgent or specialized medical care. Our mission is to ensure the safest possible transfer of young patients using advanced life-support equipment and highly trained pediatric medical teams.

    When a child’s condition requires treatment beyond local capabilities, our neonatal and pediatric medevac services enable rapid, carefully managed transport to leading children’s hospitals and neonatal intensive care units (NICUs) worldwide.

    What Is Neonatal & Pediatric Medical Transfer?

    Neonatal and pediatric medical transfer involves the specialized transportation of newborns (neonates), infants, and children who require continuous monitoring and advanced medical care during transit.

    These transfers are commonly required for:

    • ✔ Premature or critically ill newborns
    • ✔ Congenital heart or respiratory conditions
    • ✔ Severe infections or neonatal complications
    • ✔ Pediatric trauma or neurological emergencies
    • ✔ Post-surgical or ICU-level pediatric care
    • ✔ Transfer to specialized pediatric or NICU facilities

    Medevac by NEO ensures ICU-level care in the air, maintaining treatment continuity from the referring hospital to the receiving facility.

    Why Choose Medevac by NEO for Neonatal & Pediatric Transfer?

    24/7 Emergency Response

    Our neonatal and pediatric transfer services operate 24/7, enabling immediate deployment during time-critical emergencies.

    Specialized Pediatric Medical Teams

    Each mission is staffed with neonatologists, pediatric intensivists, NICU nurses, and paramedics trained specifically in newborn and child critical care.

    NICU-Equipped Air Ambulances

    Our aircraft are configured with portable incubators, neonatal ventilators, infusion pumps, cardiac monitors, oxygen systems, and temperature-controlled environments for fragile patients.

    Global Transfer Capability

    We coordinate domestic and international neonatal and pediatric transfers, connecting patients to specialized children’s hospitals across Europe, the Middle East, Asia, Africa, and the Americas.

    Family-Centered Care

    We recognize the emotional stress families face and provide clear communication, compassionate support, and guidance throughout the transfer process.

    Pediatric ICU Air Ambulance

    • ✔ Advanced life-support and medication administration

    Inter-Hospital Pediatric Transfers

    International Pediatric Medical Evacuation

    Non-Emergency Pediatric Medical Transport

    Medically supervised transport for stable pediatric patients, ideal for planned treatments or long-distance care.

    Frequently Asked Questions – Neonatal & Pediatric Transfer

    What is neonatal air ambulance transfer?

    It involves transporting critically ill or premature newborns in a fully equipped aircraft with NICU-level support and specialized neonatal medical staff.

    Is air ambulance safe for newborns and children?

    Yes. When managed by trained pediatric specialists using appropriate equipment, air ambulance transport is one of the safest transfer options.

    Do you provide international neonatal and pediatric transfers?

    Yes. Medevac by NEO provides international neonatal and pediatric medical evacuation to specialized hospitals worldwide.

    Can parents travel with the child?

    In many cases, one parent or guardian may accompany the child, subject to medical and aircraft safety considerations.

    How quickly can a transfer be arranged?

    We operate 24/7 and can arrange emergency neonatal or pediatric transfers within hours, depending on medical clearance and flight approvals.
