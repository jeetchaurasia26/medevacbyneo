    Commercial Flight Stretcher

    Medevac by Neo specializes in worldwide patient transfer using commercial airline stretcher services, supported by airline-approved medical equipment and professionally trained flight medical teams. Our goal is to make every medical journey safe, comfortable, dignified, and cost-effective.

    What Is a Commercial Flight Stretcher Service?

    A commercial flight stretcher service provides a specially arranged stretcher area onboard a scheduled commercial airline for patients who are unable to sit upright during air travel. The stretcher system and associated medical equipment are approved and installed in coordination with the airline to ensure full safety compliance.

    Medevac by Neo’s air-ambulance trained medical team accompanies the patient throughout the journey, delivering continuous monitoring, oxygen support, medication administration, and compassionate care. This option offers the same medical safety standards as a private air ambulance, but at a significantly lower cost—making it ideal for long-distance and international medical transfers.

    Ensuring Patient Privacy and Comfort

    At Medevac by Neo, patient comfort and dignity are always a priority. The commercial airline stretcher area is enclosed with privacy curtains, providing discretion and comfort throughout the flight.

    Our certified flight doctors, nurses, and paramedics closely monitor the patient’s condition in real time to ensure a stable and smooth transfer. Where permitted, family members or companions may travel on the same commercial flight, offering emotional support—an option not always available with private air ambulance services.

    Airline Stretcher Service

    When patients are unable to sit for extended periods due to illness or injury, Medevac by Neo’s airline stretcher service provides a safe and affordable alternative to chartered air ambulance flights.

    Similar to an air ambulance, we provide:

    • ✔ Trained medical escorts
    • ✔ Advanced portable medical equipment
    • ✔ Continuous in-flight monitoring
    • ✔ Oxygen support and emergency medications

    By utilizing a commercial airline rather than a dedicated aircraft, patients receive the same medical oversight and safety at a considerably lower cost.

    How Medevac by Neo Handles Every Detail

    Arranging a commercial flight stretcher transfer requires expert coordination, and Medevac by Neo manages every aspect of the process through our comprehensive bed-to-bed service.

    • ✔ Medical evaluation and eligibility assessment
    • ✔ Collection of medical reports and Fit-to-Fly certification
    • ✔ Airline approvals and stretcher booking coordination
    • ✔ Ground ambulance transfers at departure and arrival
    • ✔ Assignment of ICU-trained doctors or flight medics
    • ✔ Flight and companion ticketing arrangements
    • ✔ Continuous updates for family members throughout the journey

    Who Can Benefit from a Commercial Flight Stretcher?

    • ✔ Medically stable patients unable to sit upright for long durations
    • ✔ Post-surgical or trauma recovery patients
    • ✔ Patients requiring oxygen, IV therapy, or close monitoring
    • ✔ International medical repatriation cases
    • ✔ Those seeking a safe, comfortable, and cost-effective alternative to private air ambulance
