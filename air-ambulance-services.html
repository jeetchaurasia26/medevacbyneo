    Air Ambulance Services

    Medevac by NEO provides rapid-response air medical transport for emergencies, inter-facility transfers, and long-distance patient relocations across provinces and international borders. Our air ambulance services operate as fully equipped airborne ICUs, capable of managing complex medical conditions during flight.

    Whether the situation involves critical trauma care, organ transfer, or advanced medical treatment abroad, we ensure fast action, medical safety, and seamless coordination for urgent healthcare needs.

    Types of Air Ambulance Services

    At Medevac by NEO Worldwide, we deliver comprehensive air ambulance solutions tailored to individual patient requirements. Our global reach ensures medical evacuation services regardless of location, urgency, or medical complexity.

    Fixed Wing / Jet / Turbo Air Ambulance

    NEO specializes in fixed-wing air ambulance services designed for long-distance transfers between states and countries. Each aircraft is selected based on patient risk level, distance, and medical care requirements to ensure the highest standards of safety and efficiency.

    Our fixed-wing air ambulances provide superior comfort, speed, and medical capability, making them ideal for domestic and international evacuations.

    Why Choose Fixed-Wing Aircraft for Medical Transportation?

    At Medevac by Neo, we recognize that every second matters during medical emergencies. Our advanced fixed-wing aircraft are designed to deliver rapid, reliable, and medically supervised transport across long distances with minimal delays.

    Speed & Efficiency

    Fixed-wing air ambulances provide significantly faster transfer times for critically ill patients over long distances compared to helicopters.

    Range & Accessibility

    With extended flight range and fewer refueling stops, fixed-wing aircraft enable access to remote, cross-border, and international destinations efficiently.

    Comfort & Stability

    Pressurized and climate-controlled cabins ensure a smoother flight, essential for patients requiring continuous in-flight medical stabilization.

    Enhanced Medical Facilities

    Each aircraft functions as a flying ICU, equipped with ventilators, monitors, life-support systems, and a specialized medical team tailored to patient needs.

    Cost Effectiveness

    For long-distance transfers, fixed-wing air ambulances are more fuel-efficient and cost-effective than helicopter-based transport solutions.
