    Flight Medical Escort Service

    Safe, Comfortable, and Compassionate Care — Anywhere You Go

    At Medevac by NEO, we understand that traveling for medical reasons can be physically and emotionally exhausting—especially during illness or recovery. Our Flight Medical Escort Service is designed for patients who are stable enough to travel on commercial flights but still require professional medical supervision.

    Our trained and employed medical professionals ensure a stress-free journey, whether traveling domestically or internationally, by providing continuous care, comfort, and medical support throughout the trip.

    What’s Included in Our Medical Escort Service

    Our comprehensive medical escort solutions are tailored to each patient’s medical condition, mobility, and travel requirements, ensuring safety from departure to arrival.

    Pre-Travel Medical Evaluation

    Prior to travel, our medical team conducts a detailed consultation with the patient, their family, and attending physicians. This allows us to assess the patient’s medical condition, travel fitness, and specific care requirements.

    Based on this evaluation, a personalized medical care plan is developed to ensure safety and comfort throughout the journey.

    Professional In-Flight Medical Care

    Certified nurses or paramedics accompany patients on commercial flights to provide continuous monitoring and medical assistance. Our team is equipped to manage oxygen therapy, medication administration, and respond to unexpected medical situations.

    End-to-End Ground Transfers

    We arrange all medically assisted ground transportation, ensuring seamless bedside-to-bedside transfers. This includes hospital pick-up, airport transfers, destination coordination, and final drop-off at hospitals, rehabilitation centers, or residences.

    Travel Coordination & Logistics

    Our team manages every logistical detail of the journey, including airline medical clearance, seating arrangements (business class or stretcher if required), and coordination with airline and airport authorities to ensure smooth boarding and disembarkation.
